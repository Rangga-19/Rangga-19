About This Website

This website was created to help grandparents and the elderly community stay safe from online scams. It shares simple tips, examples and guides to protect you from tricky messages, fake calls, and unsafe websites.

Mission

My mission is to make the internet a safer place for older adults. Everyone should enjoy the benefits of being online without worrying about scams.

Why This Matters

Many elderly people lose money and personal information to scammers each year. This website aims to stop that by teaching them how to spot and avoid scams in clear, easy-to-follow steps.
